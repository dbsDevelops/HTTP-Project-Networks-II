How to Play

1. Click the big impostor to gain votes and kill him.

2. Click on the report button to rise the votes per second.

3. Click on the upgrades of the rooms to call a crewmate to help you to get votes. If a crewmate dies lowers the price.

4. Click on the vents to kick the impostors out of the room and save your crewmates

5. Click on the store button to buy some objects to your companion.
